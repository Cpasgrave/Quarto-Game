Quarto is a multi-awarded 2 player game created by Blaise MÃ¼ller, distributed by Gigamic. The 16 pieces have 4 properties: color (green or orange), shape (circle or square), height (simple or double), body (full or holed), The goal is to be the first to create a line of 4 pawns sharing one property. Player 1 chooses a pawn and gives it to player 2, player 2 places it on the board. Then P2 chooses a piece and P1 plays it. Winning alignments are rows, columns, major diagonals. In higher difficulty levels, other groups of 4 pieces than lines can be considered a win.
